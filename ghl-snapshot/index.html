What's Inside the Snapshot

Every workflow I use for Solution13 clients — from the first form fill to onboarding and re-engagement.

001.
New Lead Intake

Fires the instant a form is submitted. Instant follow-up email + internal Slack/email notification so no lead sits cold.

002.
Lead Contacted

Auto-updates the pipeline stage the moment a lead replies. No manual stage-moving, no dropped balls.

003.
Lead Qualified

Moves the opportunity to Qualified, tags the contact, and notifies the team when a lead meets your criteria.

004.
Strategy Call Booked

Confirmation email + automated 24hr and 1hr reminders. Drastically cuts no-show rates.

004b.
No-Show Recovery

3-touch re-engagement sequence over 5 days when a lead misses the call. Recovers 30–40% of no-shows.

005.
Closed Won

Triggers onboarding email sequence, alerts the team, and creates a follow-up task the moment a deal closes.

006.
Closed Lost — 30-Day Re-engagement Loop

Waits 30 days, sends a value email, then checks if the contact has since re-engaged. If yes — recycles them back into the lead pipeline. If no — clean exit. Most CRMs leave money on the table here.
