Psychology

The Science Behind the Kit

Every component maps to a behavioral economics principle. This is why it converts — not luck.

⚖️
Loss Aversion
People feel the pain of loss 2× more intensely than the pleasure of gain. Frame your CTA around what they'll miss — not what they'll get.
~2x more motivating than gain framing
👥
Social Proof
Uncertain buyers default to what others have chosen. Specificity matters — "34% lift" outperforms "amazing results" by a wide margin.
+270% conversions with reviews
🤝
Commitment & Consistency
Once someone takes a small action (scroll, click, hover), they're primed to follow through. Micro-commitments lower friction to your main CTA.
Small yes → Big yes pipeline
⏳
Scarcity
Limited availability — time or quantity — triggers urgency and reduces procrastination. Must be genuine or credibility erodes permanently.
+332% CTR with real deadlines
